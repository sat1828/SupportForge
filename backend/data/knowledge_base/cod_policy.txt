COD Policy — SupportForge Knowledge Base
=========================================

CASH ON DELIVERY AVAILABILITY
- Available for orders up to ₹10,000
- Not available for: digital goods, pre-orders, international shipments
- Available PIN codes: 18,000+ cities across India
- Minimum order value for COD: ₹199

COD CHARGES
- Orders < ₹500: ₹40 COD handling fee
- Orders ₹500–₹2,000: ₹30 COD handling fee
- Orders > ₹2,000: Free COD

PAYMENT AT DOORSTEP
- Pay exact amount to delivery agent (change availability not guaranteed)
- Accept only Indian currency
- UPI payment accepted via QR code on invoice (no COD fee applies)

COD REMITTANCE TO SELLER
- Remittance processed within 3-5 business days after delivery confirmation
- If delivery delayed beyond 7 days, contact support with order ID
- NDR (Non-Delivery Report) cases: resolved within 24-48 hours

COD FAILURE POLICIES
- 1st failure: Re-attempt next business day
- 2nd failure: Customer contacted for rescheduling
- 3rd failure in 30 days: COD suspended for 90 days
- More than 3 failures in 30 days: COD eligibility permanently reviewed

NDR (NON-DELIVERY REPORT) HANDLING
- Raised when delivery agent cannot deliver
- Reasons: Customer unavailable, wrong address, refused delivery
- Resolution: Customer receives SMS/call within 2 hours
- If customer claims delivery occurred but NDR raised: escalated to supervisor

REFUND FOR COD ORDERS
- Returned COD orders: Refund via NEFT to registered bank account
- Bank account must be verified (takes 24h)
- Processing: 7-10 business days after QC approval
