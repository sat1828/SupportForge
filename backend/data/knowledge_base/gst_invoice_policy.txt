GST Invoice Policy — SupportForge Knowledge Base
================================================

OVERVIEW
Our platform issues GST-compliant invoices for all taxable transactions. This document
outlines invoice generation, GSTIN validation, and refund eligibility rules.

GSTIN VALIDATION
- Format: 2-digit state code + 10-digit PAN + 1-digit entity number + 1-digit Z + 1 checksum
- Example valid GSTIN: 29ABCDE1234F1Z5
- Invalid GSTINs will result in invoice rejection and require re-submission
- Customers must provide GSTIN at checkout for B2B transactions

GST SLABS (FY 2024-25)
- 0%: Essential goods (rice, wheat, books, contraceptives)
- 5%: Packaged food, footwear < ₹1000, apparel < ₹1000
- 12%: Processed food, mobiles, computers
- 18%: Most electronics, software, restaurant services
- 28%: Luxury goods, automobiles, tobacco

INVOICE GENERATION
- Invoices generated within 24 hours of order confirmation
- Available in customer portal under "My Orders > Invoice"
- Format: PDF with QR code for verification
- Validity: Permanent (no expiry)

REFUND ELIGIBILITY
- Refund claims accepted up to 2 years from invoice date
- Claims beyond 2 years are automatically rejected (system rule)
- Refund amount cannot exceed original invoice amount including GST
- Partial refunds allowed for partial returns

INVOICE DISPUTES
- Raise dispute within 30 days of invoice generation
- Corrections allowed: customer name, GSTIN, shipping address
- Amount corrections require cancellation and re-issue (takes 3-5 business days)

DOWNLOAD INSTRUCTIONS
1. Login to portal → My Orders → Select Order → Download Invoice
2. If invoice unavailable after 24h, contact support with order ID
3. Bulk download available for business accounts (up to 100 invoices at once)
