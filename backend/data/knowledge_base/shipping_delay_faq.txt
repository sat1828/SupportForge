Shipping Delay FAQ — SupportForge Knowledge Base
=================================================

STANDARD DELIVERY TIMELINES
- Metro cities (Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, Kolkata): 1-2 business days
- Tier-2 cities: 2-4 business days
- Tier-3 cities / Rural: 4-7 business days
- Remote areas (J&K, Northeast, islands): 7-14 business days

WHY IS MY ORDER DELAYED?
Common reasons for shipping delays:
1. High order volume (festive season, sale events)
2. Weather disruptions (floods, cyclones, fog)
3. Courier partner operational issues
4. Address verification pending
5. Customs clearance (for cross-border items)
6. Public holidays in origin/destination state

HOW TO TRACK YOUR ORDER
1. Login → My Orders → Click order → View Tracking
2. SMS updates sent to registered mobile
3. Courier tracking link sent via email after dispatch
4. WhatsApp updates available (opt-in)

WHEN TO RAISE A COMPLAINT
- Metro: Delay beyond 3 business days from dispatch
- Other cities: Delay beyond 7 business days from dispatch
- Rural: Delay beyond 14 business days from dispatch

COMPENSATION FOR DELAYS
- 1-3 day delay: ₹50 coupon code
- 3-7 day delay: ₹150 coupon OR 5% refund on order value
- >7 day delay: Full refund option OR ₹300 coupon
- Perishable items: Full refund if delayed beyond 1 day

DAMAGED IN TRANSIT
- Photograph damage before opening package
- Report within 48 hours of delivery
- Replacement dispatched within 2 business days (subject to stock)
- Original product must be returned in provided return packaging

LOST IN TRANSIT
- Raised after 3 business days beyond maximum timeline
- Investigation takes 3-5 business days
- Full refund or replacement after investigation
