Escalation SLA Policy — SupportForge Knowledge Base
====================================================

PRIORITY LEVELS AND SLA TARGETS

P1 — CRITICAL (Response: 15 min | Resolution: 1 hour)
Triggers:
- Financial loss > ₹50,000
- Data breach or security incident
- Payment gateway complete outage
- Legal/regulatory threat received
Action: Immediate escalation to senior manager + legal team

P2 — HIGH (Response: 1 hour | Resolution: 4 hours)
Triggers:
- Financial impact ₹10,000–₹50,000
- Fraud detected (GST mismatch, refund fraud)
- Multiple customers affected by same issue
- SLA breach imminent
Action: Escalate to team lead, block further automated processing

P3 — MEDIUM (Response: 4 hours | Resolution: 24 hours)
Triggers:
- Standard refund request > ₹5,000
- COD remittance pending > 7 days
- Warranty claim disputes
- Account deactivation appeals
Action: Senior agent review required

P4 — LOW (Response: 24 hours | Resolution: 72 hours)
Triggers:
- General information queries
- Minor product feedback
- Subscription changes
Action: Standard agent handling

SLA BREACH CONSEQUENCES
- P1 breach: Automatic escalation to VP Customer Experience
- P2 breach: Escalation to department head + compensation trigger
- P3/P4 breach: Manager review + customer apology coupon

ESCALATION TRIGGERS IN AI SYSTEM
The AI will automatically escalate when:
1. Confidence score < 65% after 3 KB searches
2. Fraud signals detected (any severity)
3. Refund amount > ₹5,000
4. Customer uses legal language (FIR, consumer forum, court)
5. Execution budget exceeded (system protection)
6. All LLM providers unavailable
