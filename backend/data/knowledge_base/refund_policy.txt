Refund Policy — SupportForge Knowledge Base
============================================

STANDARD REFUND TIMELINES
- Credit/Debit Card: 5-7 business days
- UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm): 2-3 business days
- Net Banking: 5-7 business days
- COD (Cash on Delivery): NEFT to registered bank account within 7-10 business days
- Wallet Credits: Instant (if refunded to platform wallet)

REFUND ELIGIBILITY CONDITIONS
✅ Eligible:
- Product not delivered within promised timeframe
- Wrong product delivered
- Damaged/defective product received
- Order cancelled before dispatch
- Duplicate payment charged

❌ Not Eligible:
- Digital products after download/activation
- Perishable goods after delivery
- Customized/personalized items
- Products returned without original packaging (>50% damage)
- Claims submitted after 30 days of delivery

PARTIAL REFUNDS
- Allowed when: partial items missing, minor damage, late delivery compensation
- Amount: determined by customer care based on evidence
- Processing time: same as full refund timelines

REFUND PROCESS
1. Raise refund request within 30 days of delivery
2. Submit photos/evidence if product damaged
3. Pickup scheduled within 2-3 business days
4. Quality check at warehouse: 2-3 business days
5. Refund initiated after QC approval

HIGH-VALUE REFUNDS (> ₹5,000)
- Require manual review by senior customer care
- Additional verification may be requested
- Processing time: 7-10 business days
- Admin approval required before processing

FRAUD PREVENTION
- More than 2 refund requests in 30 days triggers review
- Refund amount exceeding invoice total is automatically blocked
- Address change after order placement flags for manual review
